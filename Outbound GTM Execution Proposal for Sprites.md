# Proposal: **Outbound GTM Execution for Sprites.ai**

## **Why Now**

Sprites.ai has early proof of value and first successful customers, and now needs to scale outbound for higher-value accounts to 1\) validate ICP and 2\) support a Series A raise targeted for June.

You’ve got a working product across Meta \+ Google (plus SEO), and you’re already closing customer deals.

Now the goal is to turn those early wins into a repeatable outbound motion with both revenue momentum and, ideally, a few prestige logos for the Series A story. 

## **Your Goals**

1. Validate 3-5 ICP hypotheses quickly and decide what to double down on (3-week experiment window)  
2. Add a LinkedIn outbound channel to go along with cold email    
3. Test both motions:  
   * champion-led (marketing/ad manager → head, C-level)  
   * top-down (C-level → marketing team)  
4. Prioritize segments where unit economics make sense, especially:  
   * PE rollups / multi-brand operators (per-brand pricing expansion)  
   * higher-value SMBs with strong signals  
5. Increase outbound email bandwidth via solid warmed infrastructure.

## **Proposed Solution by Voitech**

We will set up and execute a LinkedIn outbound engine that’s designed specifically for Sprites.ai’s ICP testing phase: narrow hypotheses, controlled variables, fast learning, and transparent output.

You can expect the first demos booked in Month 2, and by end of Month 3 customers can typically determine which ICP and messaging deserve larger focus.

We’ll first run structured account sourcing, segmentation and research in Clay/n8n to formulate a single database of validated ICP-fit companies. Then we'll formulate hypotheses together with your team and execute outreach through LinkedIn and email automation platforms.

Our vision is to run segmented outreach with pre-designed copy elements to measure what's working with light signal-based personalization, and reserve deeper work for Tier 1 accounts only.

## **Sprints & Timelines**

### **Week 1\. Onboarding \+ Infrastructure \+ Research Setup**

**What we do**

* Align on what ICP test success means \+ define weekly work cycles.  
* Research ICP \+ buyer personas and build targeting rules per segment:  
  * founder-led SMB  
  * PE rollups / multi-brand operators  
  * traditional services businesses  
  * prestige brands (for Series A narrative)  
* Product onboarding and internal demo(s): value props, objections, proof points.  
* Review current customers and study their stories in depth.  
* Set up an initial Clay research pipeline (enrichment, validation, tagging, signals).  
* LinkedIn profile setup, warmup launch.  
* Tool stack setup: domains \+ email infrastructure (if used), analytics dashboards, outreach tooling.  
* Build the first draft of the account list (starting cohort).

**Deliverables**

* **ICP \+ buyer persona definitions** (v1.0) \+ signals library (what counts as ‘strong fit’).  
* **Account list for 1 ICP cohort** (v1.0), enriched \+ tagged in Clay.  
* **LinkedIn profiles prepared** \+ warmup launched (ready for outbound in Week 4).  
* **Product context document** for outbound: use cases, positioning, FAQs, customer results.

### **Weeks 2-3. Hypothesis Design \+ Sequences \+ List Building**

**What we do**

* Convert **3 ICP hypotheses** into testable cohorts.  
* Create LinkedIn message sequences per cohort:  
  * Version A/B  
  * bottom-up vs top-down variants where relevant  
* Build lists in Clay: enrichment \+ QA \+ segmentation tags.  
* Implement account tiering system to allocate effort correctly:  
  * Tier 1 (whales / rollups / prestige)  
  * Tier 2 (scale targets)  
  * Tier 3 (low priority / high volume)  
* Prepare outbound playbook: process rules, reply handling, handoffs, logging.

**Deliverables**

* **3 hypotheses packaged as cohorts** (targeting rules \+ expected outcomes).  
* **Sequences for each cohort** (A/B versions ready to launch).  
* **Cohort lists built in Clay** (v1.0), enriched \+ QA’d \+ tiered.  
* **Outbound playbook (v1.0)**: targeting → outreach → replies → next steps.

### **Weeks 4-5. Soft Launch → Scale Winners**

**What we do**

* Launch **2–3 cohorts** with a gradual ramp in connection volume (quality \> block).  
* Monitor engagement signals daily and review weekly with the team:  
  * connect rate  
  * reply rate  
  * “right person / wrong person” patterns  
* Refine hypotheses and research pipelines based on real responses.  
* Define lead handling rules upon reply (qualifying questions, routing, “sell-up” motion).

**Deliverables**

* **Weekly performance report** by cohort (baseline metrics \+ early signal).  
* **Iteration pack**: updated targeting rules \+ updated sequences (v1.1).  
* **Reply handling rules (v1.0)**: qualification flow \+ routing \+ follow-up structure.  
* **Tier 1 whale motion initialized** (small list, higher personalization).

### **Week 6+. Ongoing Outreach \+ Continuous Hypothesis Testing**

**What we do**

* Deliver a new hypothesis every **1-2 weeks**  
  Target: **\~8 hypotheses tested over 3 months** (fast learning, narrow scope).  
* Weekly iteration on:  
  * list filters / signals  
  * messaging hooks  
  * persona approach (champion vs boss)  
* Expand the Tier 1 whale motion separately (high-value accounts only).

**Deliverables**

* **1 new cohort every 1–2 weeks** (list \+ sequence \+ targeting rules).  
* **Weekly performance \+ learnings report** (what worked / what didn’t / next experiment).  
* **Ongoing list refresh \+ enrichment** (keeping cohorts high-signal).  
* **Playbook upgrades** (v1.2 → v1.3): objections, proof points, qualification notes, handoffs.

### **Signals**

Given our current level of understanding, account scoring will focus on a small set of **high-signal indicators** that correlate with both *fit* and *buying intent*:

* **Multi-brand or PE rollup activity**: parent company managing multiple sub-brands, centralized growth function)  
* **Clear performance marketing footprint**: visible paid acquisition focus, always-on campaigns, frequent creative changes  
* **Growth \+ execution pressure**: headcount growth, new market launches, expansion plans  
* **Marketing team structure**: founder-led vs specialist team; presence of a growth lead / performance marketer  
* **Operational complexity**: multiple websites/brands, fragmented execution, signs of heavy reliance on agencies

As we prepare hypotheses during Month 1, we’ll expand and refine the signal set per ICP and prioritize signals by strength \+ intent, so targeting becomes tighter with every test cycle.
