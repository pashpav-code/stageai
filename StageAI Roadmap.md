## Целевые метрики (reference)

### M1 · Connect test (W3–4 · 15–28 июн)

- Connection requests: 50–80 (mid 65)
- **Connect rate: 40% → 26 accepts**
- Meetings / SQO: 0 (no pitch)

### M2 · Targeted seg.1+2 (W5–9 · 29 июн – 2 авг)

- Messaged: ~80 (40 seg.1 + 40 seg.2)
- Reply rate: 30% → 24 replies
- **Meetings: 8 · SQO: 2 (≈ 30%) · Pipeline: €80k** @ avg $40k ARR

### M3 · Scale + close (W10–13 · 3 – 30 авг)

- Contacts: 400–500 (20–25 co/нед × 4 нед · 5 contacts each)
- Connect rate: 40% → 160 connects
- Reply rate: 30% (of messaged) → 48 replies
- **Meetings: 15 · New SQO: +6 (8 total cumulative) · Pipeline: €320k**

### Cumulative 13 weeks

- **23 meetings · 8 SQO · €320k pipeline** @ avg $40k ARR · ~30% SQO conversion

### Capacity

- **LinkedIn:** 3 accounts → 1 000–1 500 contacts / мес
- **Email:** 25/inbox/day · 4 emails/lead/3wk → 4–5 inbox (test) · 10 (scale)

## Single-account context (шаблон)

CRM + `artifacts/<account-slug>/` или Supabase:

```text
account_id, domain, segment (W1/W3), tier (T1-T3)
hypothesis_id, persona_primary (P1/P2/P3)
research_summary, pov_version, soundbite
touches[]: {date, channel, type, message_id, outcome}
replies[]: {date, sentiment, objection, next_step}
meetings[], champion, economic_buyer
ml_signoff, exec_sponsor, notes
```

**Правило:** обновление ≤ 24h после каждого touch и каждого call.
